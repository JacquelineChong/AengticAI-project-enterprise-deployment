Data Transformation Playbook for SMEs

A practical guide for small and medium enterprises to build data capabilities with limited resources and developing skillsets

About This Playbook

This playbook is designed specifically for Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) embarking on their data transformation journey with limited resources, budget constraints, and emerging technical capabilities. While we use a Food & Beverage business case as our primary example, the principles, frameworks, and implementation strategies outlined here are applicable across various industries.

Unlike enterprise-scale digital transformation guides that assume substantial resources and mature technical teams, this playbook acknowledges the reality most SMEs face: the need to build data capabilities incrementally while demonstrating business value at each step. We focus on practical, actionable approaches that balance technical best practices with the constraints of smaller organizations.

The playbook walks through establishing a data lake as your foundation, creating cross-disciplinary business domains, implementing appropriate governance structures, and measuring success - all tailored to organizations with developing data maturity. Whether you're a business leader seeking to understand the potential of data-driven decision making or a technical manager tasked with implementing these capabilities, this guide provides a clear roadmap to follow.
